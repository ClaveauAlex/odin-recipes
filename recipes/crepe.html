Crêpe Complète

Description

The whole pancake is a must of the savory pancakes. It has given birth to many variations, nothing can match it with its mixture of ham, grated cheese and egg.

Steps

  1. Put some butter into wood burner.
  2. Then, you can put carefully your crepe inside.
  3. I already put an egg above.
  4. Finally, you can put anything you want around the center of your crepe.
